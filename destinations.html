Travel Stories — Destinations

Kyoto, Japan

A peaceful city of temples, traditional wooden machiya houses and seasonal gardens — perfect for slow exploration.

Highlights / Activities:
  • Visit Fushimi Inari and walk the torii path
  • Tea ceremony in a historic teahouse
  • Stroll the Arashiyama bamboo grove

Santorini, Greece

Iconic whitewashed villages clinging to caldera cliffs — sunsets here are the stuff of postcards.

Highlights / Activities:
  • Watch the sunset in Oia over the caldera
  • Wine tasting at a local vineyard
  • Boat trip to the volcanic islets

Queenstown, New Zealand

Adventure capital surrounded by dramatic mountains and clear lakes — ideal for thrill-seekers and nature lovers.

Highlights / Activities:
  • Bungee jump or try the zipline
  • Hike the Ben Lomond Track
  • Relax on a Milford Sound cruise
